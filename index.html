About/Mission & Vision

Our vision is to create a hub of challenging learning environment that encourage high expectations and success for every learner/child. And our mission is to hearnest the full potentials of our students by providing a high quality, comprehensive and meaningful education to all. We enroll both Nursery and Lower Basic students. We partake in external examinations such as The National Assessment Test (NAT), and The Gambia Basic Examination Certificate (GABECE).
